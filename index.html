Mental health


Mental health is an important part of overall health and well-being. According to the National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI),1 one in five adults in the United States experiences a mental illness in a given year. Mental illness can range from mild to severe, and it can impact any aspect of life, including work, relationships, and physical health. Mental illness can be treated with medication and/or therapy. For some people, medication is the only treatment that is effective, while others find that therapy alone works best for them.

Some people with mental illness may need to try different treatments before they find what works best for them. If you are struggling with a mental illness, it is important to seek help.
